HOUSE OF SCALES

Suppose we had a nice, small box of a house. If we multiplied the width by 10, how much would the total volume increase?

Press the button to find out.

Use the dimensions of the little house (LH) and the 10:1 scale to determine the dimensions of the big house (BH). Fill in the chart and then decorate the house!

Clicking on the up arrow at the bottom left moves the house down so you can see inside it. Clicking on the down arrow moves it back to its original position. The left and right arrows rotate the house in opposite directions.

Remember, use the 10 to 1 scale factor and multiplication to fill in the chart. You'll need to think about the formulas for area and for volume, too.

As you fill in the right answer, menus will appear at the bottom of the page. You can use them to decorate the house. To see the inside of the house close up, right click on a PC or control click on a Mac, and zoom in.
